I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Engineering at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Y. Charlie Hu. My current research interest is in networked and mobile systems.

Previously, I got my M.S. from UCLA Computer Science, working at the Internet Research Lab under the supervision of Prof. Lixia Zhang in 2020. I got my B.S. from School of Automation and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University in 2018.
