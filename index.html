About Me

I work on Llama post-training infra at Meta Superintelligence Labs. I was previously on the AI and Systems Co-Design team.

I received my PhD from Purdue University in 2025, working on computer systems and was advised by Prof. Y. Charlie Hu. My PhD research has focused on (1) Building efficient, high-throughput machine learning inference systems, with a focus on supporting latency-critical applications such as augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR). (2) Experimental measurement studies on emerging mobile networks such as 5G. My research has been acknowledged with a Best Paper Award from ACM EdgeSys 2022, and a Best Dataset Award from PAM 2021.
